1. Information We Collect · 我们收集的信息

We collect anonymous device identifiers, session duration, emotional state selections, and attention ratings entered by parents or therapists. We do NOT collect names, photos, or location data.

我们收集匿名设备标识符、练习时长、情绪状态选择及家长/治疗师输入的注意力评分。我们不收集姓名、照片或位置数据。

2. Children's Privacy (COPPA) · 儿童隐私保护

Joy of Guqin is designed for use with children under supervision of a parent or therapist. We comply with COPPA. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. All data is anonymous.

愉悦古琴专为在家长或治疗师监督下使用的儿童设计。我们遵守COPPA法规，不收集13岁以下儿童的个人信息。所有数据均为匿名。

3. How We Use Information · 信息使用方式

Data is used solely for in-app progress reports and anonymized music therapy research. We do not sell or share data with third parties for marketing.

数据仅用于应用内进度报告及匿名音乐疗愈研究，不出售或共享给第三方用于营销。

4. Data Storage · 数据存储

Session data is stored locally on device and backed up anonymously to JSONBin.io. Data is retained for maximum 12 months.

会话数据存储在本地设备，并匿名备份至JSONBin.io，最长保留12个月。
